Who We Are

An independent studio for a very particular art.

Alpha Centauri began as a solo pursuit and grew into a creative collective — four distinct identities, one shared language. We create fantasy spanking art through the lens of anime and manga, combining AI-assisted tools with disciplined human creative direction.

Every piece starts with a story. Every story starts with a character. And behind every character is one of us — working late, imagining worlds, refining details that most people will never consciously notice, but will certainly feel.
